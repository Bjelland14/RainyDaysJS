Frequently Asked Questions

What is RainyDays?

RainyDays is a Norwegian outdoor brand focused on durable, stylish rain jackets designed for Scandinavian weather.

What does WCR mean?

WCR stands for Water Column Rating – the waterproof measurement of our jackets.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are normally delivered within 3–5 business days in Norway.

Can I return a product?

Yes, you can return items within 30 days provided they are unused and with tags.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach us via email at support@rainydays.no.
